Tetiana Dushenkivska is the founder of Rozoomio and the creator of ElixirCards. With over 10 years of experience building systems on the BEAM, she works with Elixir, Phoenix, and LiveView to design and ship real-time, production-grade applications.
Alongside her client work, Tetiana is building learning tools that combine cognitive science, gamification, and AI to help developers learn more effectively and avoid burnout.
