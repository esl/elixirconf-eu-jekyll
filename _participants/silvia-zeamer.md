Silvia Zeamer is a Software Engineer at Tolemi, working on data pipelines and infrastructure using Elixir, Postgres, Python, and other tools as needs dictate. She studied Media Arts and Sciences at Wellesley College.
She has spent much of her life living on an off-grid homestead in coastal Maine, tackling a variety of interesting electrical problems but is now based in Boston, MA, where she spends her free time binding books, photographing nature, and designing knitwear.
