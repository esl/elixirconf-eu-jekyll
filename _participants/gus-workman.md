Gus is a Nerves core team member, and is the founder of Protolux Electronics, a Luxembourg-based company specializing in hardware and software for embedded systems built with Nerves. Additionally, he hosts the Nerves Meetup EU monthly online meetup for hardware and embedded discussions in the Elixir ecosystem.
