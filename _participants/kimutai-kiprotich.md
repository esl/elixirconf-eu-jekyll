Kiprotich Kimutai is CTO of Nexuscale.ai, where he built AI-powered sales automation that manages 200+ million leads and delivers 3.2x conversion improvements. With 5+ years building production Elixir applications, he specializes in fault-tolerant AI systems at scale.

Kim architected Nexuscale from the ground up, achieving 94% targeting accuracy for B2B sales teams. The platform launched on AppSumo, scaled to handle massive traffic, and now serves 1,000+ users generating significant revenue.
He's delivered AI solutions across ticketing systems, healthcare, and other industries using Elixir's Nx library for machine learning. His expertise spans Phoenix LiveView frontends to OTP-supervised AI workflows, focusing on real-time processing and concurrent AI orchestration.

Based in Nairobi, Kenya, Kim is passionate about making AI automation accessible through robust Elixir applications.
