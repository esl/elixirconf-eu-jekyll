ABOUT THE CONFERENCE

ElixirConfÂ® EU Virtual 2020 was an online two-track conference with 30-minute speaking slots with optional Q&A.

The event was run from 2pm to 7pm CEST (8am EDT to 1pm EDT) on both days.

WELCOME

The premiere Elixir conference in Europe went Virtual!

The ElixirConf EU conference is a melting pot for new ideas and inspired thinking. It helps those new to the community find their feet, and welcomes back those who have attended before, to champion and progress the uptake and development of Elixir in Europe.

Since we couldn't meet in person in April, we've brought the ElixirConf EU feel to your home in June.

All the published videos are available on YouTube.
