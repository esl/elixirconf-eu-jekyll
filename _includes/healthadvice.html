Health Advice

The health, safety and wellbeing of all visitors to our events is of the utmost importance. We are currently monitoring the effect of the outbreak of coronavirus disease (COVID-19) upon our conferences and would like to let you know that ElixirConf EU 2020 is still going ahead. If that changes we will notify all ticket holders immediately and post a further statement here.

If it is necessary to cancel ElixirConf EU, we will issue a full refund to everyone attending. We will not be able to refund any travel or accommodation costs and recommend that all visitors take out appropriate travel insurance to cover any cancellations due to events outside our control. Contact us if you have any further concerns.
