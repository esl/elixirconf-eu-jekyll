CALL FOR TALKS


ElixirConf® EU 2022 is a two-track conference with 40-minute speaking slots including optional Q&A.

This year we’re accepting talks from people who are happy to deliver them in-person or virtually from anywhere in the world (though keep in mind the Central European time zone). We are looking for speakers with Elixir-centric content and also speakers who can bring pragmatic and practical knowledge into their Elixir talks. It doesn't matter if you are new to Elixir or have multiple years on a core team. We look forward to what you can share with the global Elixir community. CFT deadline: 9th December 2021

    Below are some topics to get your creative juices flowing:
  • Adoption and/or leveraging of Elixir
  • Elixir Ecosystems: Phoenix, Ecto, Nerves, Scenic, LiveView
  • Deployment: Tools, strategies, and stories
  • Distributed Systems: Presence, Broadway, multi-node, and stories
  • In the Wild: Elixir in Web, Gaming, Medical, Data science, etc
  • Elixir for Corporate Computing, Small Businesses or Startups
  • Tools and Testing: Methodologies, IDEs, and strategies
  • Related Topics (BEAM, ETS, OTP, CRDT, Compiler)
