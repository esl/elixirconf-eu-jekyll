CALL FOR TALKS


ElixirConf® EU 2026 in Málaga is a two-track hybrid conference with 20- and 40-minute speaking slots including optional Q&A.

Do you have knowledge or experience with Elixir and its rapidly evolving ecosystem? We're seeking speakers focused on the areas where **Elixir and high-impact application development** truly shine, exploring advanced techniques, real-world scaling challenges, and the integration of emerging technologies. We seek talks that are technically deep, practical, and heavily oriented toward building **robust, maintainable, and highly concurrent, distributed systems**.
Submit your talk proposal by 6 January.

    Need talk ideas? We're looking for advanced content across these core areas:
  • Core Phoenix & Application Architecture: Deep dives into *Phoenix Contexts and Boundaries*, advanced strategies for **managing application state**, *Ecto testing*, comprehensive **LiveView testing** patterns, and performance optimization for I/O bound tasks.
  • The Modern Web & Data Landscape: Beyond-the-basics *Advanced LiveView Architectures*, including *complex component design*, *optimizing the browser-server communication*, and *JavaScript interop*. Practical applications of *Elixir in GenAI & Machine Learning* using **Nx**, and high-throughput data pipelines with *Broadway, Flow, and Oban* via **Ecto**.
  • Ecosystem, Operations, and Future Trends: Compelling **Real-World Case Studies & ROI** detailing business value and lessons learned from scaling with Phoenix and **Nerves**. Best practices for **Modern DevOps & Observability** (Kubernetes, Nomad), advanced **Telemetry** and tracing, and improving the Elixir *Developer Experience (DX)*.
